Classic Dave's Burger Recipe

A juicy whiskey-infused burger that's sure to impress.

Ingredients:
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/4 cup whiskey. Full cup for the brave.
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Burger buns and desired toppings (lettuce, tomato, cheese, etc.)
